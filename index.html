About Lucy.

✨ Hi, welcome to my website! ✨

I love helping people create beautiful living spaces!

I graduated from LCI Melbourne with a Bachelor of Arts in Interior Design.

I'm currently studying a Diploma of Building and Construction at RMIT to broaden my construction and project management knowledge.

I love learning new things, and I always give 110%!

After finishing VCE I took a gap year to immerse myself in the world of art and design, inspiring my designs for years to come.

I am passionate about art and design, and I love colourful things. I am always seeking new opportunities to learn.

When I’m not designing, I enjoy looking after my plants, re-arranging my home, and going on breakfast dates with my partner.
